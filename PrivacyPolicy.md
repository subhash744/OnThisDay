# OnThisDay APP Privacy Policy

**Date of last update:** June 19, 2026

OnThisDay does not collects, processes, and shares none of your personal data. The app does not have an account, has no server, nor any backend.

The app obtains historical events information from the publicly accessible API of Wikipedia. Your bookmarked events are saved locally on your device and are never shared anywhere else.

Ads and third-party data collection are not yet implemented in this application.

This app is based on content that is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 license (CC BY-SA 4.0). Any images from Wikimedia Commons used in this app are subject to a different license; please refer to the Wikipedia page of each event for its respective image license.
